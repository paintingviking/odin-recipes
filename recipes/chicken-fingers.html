Quick and easy chicken fingers!

Need a quick and easy meal that everyone likes, and can be eaten both hot and cold, with or without dipping sauce? Look no further!

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts
  • salt
  • pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Bread crumbs
  • oil
  1. Cut chicken into 2cm wide strips
  2. Add seasoning to bread crumbs
  3. Put pan on stove on medium-high heat and add a good amount of Oil
  4. Put chicken strups into flour, then egg and finally into the bread crumbs
  5. Fry until golden and crispy on one side, then turn them over
  6. Put the finished strips on a paper towel to wick away any excess oil
  7. Serve with dipping sauce of your choice!
