Really good chili-con-carne!

Fast and easy comfort meal that makes for perfect leftovers!

Ingredients:

  • 1 large onion
  • 1 red pepper
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • Oil
  • 1 tsp hot chili powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp dried marjoram
  • 300ml water
  • 500g lean minced beef
  • 1 beef stock cube
  • 400g can of chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp tomato purée
  • 400g can red kidney beans
  • plain boiled long grain rice
  • Sour cream
  1. Prep vegetables. Chop onion into cubes
  2. Cut the red pepper in half, remove seeds then chop. Peel and finely chop the garlic cloves
  3. Put pan on stove over medium heat and add oil
  4. Add onion and cook until soft
  5. Add garlic, red pepper, chili powder, paprika and cumin
  6. Give a good stir, then cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally
  7. Brown the ground beef
  8. Stir and prod the minced beef until uniform and no more pink lumps
  9. Make the sauce. Add beef cube to 300ml hot water. Add to beef mix
  10. Add the chopped tomatoes, marjoram, sugar, some salt and pepper and add the tomatoe purée
  11. Bring to a boil, give it a good stir. Turn down the heat and add a lid to keep it simmering and leave for 20 minutes
  12. Stir occasionally to keep from sticking to the bottom or isn't drying out. If it is, add some water and check the heat. Should look thick, moist and juicy.
  13. Strain the kidney beans and add to the pot. Bring to a quick boil, then reduce to a simmer for 10 minutes without a lid, adding water if it looks to dry
  14. Taste and add more seasoning if needed
  15. Replace lid, turn off heat and let rest for 10 minutes
  16. Serve with rice and sour cream
